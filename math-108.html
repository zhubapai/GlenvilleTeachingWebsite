MATH 108: Intermediate Algebra

Continues the algebra sequence with quadratic equations, rational and radical expressions, complex numbers, functions and their graphs, and an introduction to exponential and logarithmic functions. Prerequisite: MATH 106 with a "C" or higher, or placement.
