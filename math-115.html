MATH 115: College Algebra

A study of algebraic equations, inequalities, graphs, and functions — including linear, quadratic, absolute value, rational, radical, polynomial, exponential, and logarithmic functions. Prerequisite: ACT Math 21 or higher, SAT Math 530 or higher, or MATH 108 with a "C" or higher.
