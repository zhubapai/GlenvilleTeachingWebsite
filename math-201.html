MATH 201: Introduction to Reasoning & Proof

A transition course bridging computational mathematics and proof-based reasoning: logic, set theory, and methods of proof (direct, contrapositive, contradiction, and induction), along with relations, functions, and cardinality. Prerequisite: MATH 115 or equivalent.
