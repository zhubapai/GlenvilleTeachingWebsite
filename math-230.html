MATH 230: Euclidean Geometry for College Students

An axiomatic development of Euclidean geometry, covering points, lines, and planes, congruence and similarity, circles, area and volume, geometric constructions, and an introduction to non-Euclidean geometry. Prerequisite: MATH 115 or equivalent.
